Report Copyright or Trademark Infringement
Square respects the copyright and trademark rights of others and asks that our customers do the same. As such, we have adopted a Copyright and Trademark Policy with respect to third-party material that infringes the copyrights or trademarks of others and we respond to all valid notices of infringement.

If you believe that third-party material posted on or accessible through Square’s applications, websites, and services constitutes infringement of your copyright or trademark rights, please send a notice of infringement to Square’s designated agent by filing a report here.

While the online form is the easiest and quickest way to submit your complaint with us, if you prefer, you may also write to Square’s designated agent at the following address. If you write to us, please be sure to provide all the necessary information. Please review our Copyright and Trademark Policy here to learn what information we will need from you.

Square, Inc. 
Attn: Copyright/Trademark Agent 
1455 Market Street, Suite 600 
San Francisco, CA 94103
