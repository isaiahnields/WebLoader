Square Dashboard Reporting for eCommerce API

Article Contents

View eCommerce Sales in Sales Summary
View eCommerce Transaction History
With Square’s reporting tools, you can view your API transaction history from your online Square Dashboard.

View eCommerce Sales in Sales Summary

To filter eCommerce transactions in your Sales Summary:

From your Dashboard, click the Sales tab.
From the Reports tab > Sales Summary.
Click on Advanced Options.
From the drop-down list, select Source.
Click on All Sources > filter dropdown and select eCommerce Integrations.


Note: The All Sources filter dropdown lists available sources based on the selected transaction data. If the transaction results are empty, the dropdown will be empty. If the transaction results only include Point of Sale transactions, the dropdown will only list “Point of Sale”.

View eCommerce Transaction History

To filter eCommerce transactions in your transaction history:

From your online Square Dashboard, click Sales.
Select Transactions.
Select eCommerce Integrations from the All Payment Methods dropdown.
