Get Started With the Square Dashboard App

Article Contents

Download the Square Dashboard App
Sign In to the Square Dashboard App
The Square Dashboard app is compatible with iPhones running the latest version of iOS software. Download the app and generate reports to get a snapshot of your business – daily sales summary, category and item sales – on the go.

Note: The Dashboard app isn’t compatible with iPads and Android devices at this time, but this is something we’re working on! 

Download the Square Dashboard App

Tap the App Store icon on your iPhone.
Tap the search icon (magnifying glass) on the bottom navigation bar and enter Square Dashboard.
Tap Get next to the Dashboard app icon:
Follow the prompts to download the app.
Sign In to the Square Dashboard App

From your iPhone, open the Dashboard app.
Enter the email address and password associated to your Square account.
Tap Sign In.
If you’ve forgotten your password, you can reset it from the app or from your online Square Dashboard at any time.
